Voice is an intelligent issue reporting and tracking system that empowers users to effortlessly 
raise concerns via voice input through a mobile app. Administrators can efficiently manage and 
resolve these issues using a powerful web dashboard. Powered by advanced AI, Voice offers smart 
classification and actionable insights, streamlining problem resolution and enhancing communication.
